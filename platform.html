FAQs

Frequently asked questions
What is the CBE platform?
The CBE platform is a computer-based exam platform that allows you to take your exams online. It is a user-friendly platform that is designed to revolutionize your exam-taking experience.
How do I access the CBE platform for my exams?
To access our CBE platform, simply click on the “Go to CBE platform” and sign up or login using your credentials. Once logged in, navigate to the designated exam section, select the desired exam, and begin your exam session seamlessly.
Can I use any device to take exams on the CBE platform?
Yes you can! The CBE platform is compatible with all devices, including desktops, laptops, tablets, and mobile phones.
What happens if I experience technical issues during my exam?
If you experience any technical issues during your exam, please contact our support team immediately. Our team will work to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.
Can I review and change my answers before submitting my exam?
Of course! You can review and change your answers at any time before submitting your exam.
How long will I have access to my exam results on the CBE platform?
You will have access to your exam results for 30 days after the exam date. After 30 days, your exam results will no longer be available on the platform.
